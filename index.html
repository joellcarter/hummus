Our Promise

Three things we never compromise.

Authentic

Every ingredient — from our hummus and tahini to our olive oil — is sourced directly from the Mediterranean. The flavors you taste are the exact flavors served in village restaurants across the region.

Fresh

Our hummus and falafel are prepared from scratch every single morning. We source most of our produce from local farms. Nothing on your plate was made yesterday.

Healthy

We use only premium natural ingredients and never add preservatives or substitutes. Whether you are vegan, vegetarian, or simply curious, every dish here is honest food made with care.

The Hummus Village Plate

Our signature plate — rich hummus made from scratch, crowned with warm chickpeas, fresh parsley, and Mediterranean olive oil. Served with crisp Israeli salad, house pickles, and warm pita bread. This is the dish that started everything.
